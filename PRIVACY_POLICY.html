Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all apps published by Matt Callihan on the Amazon App Store and other app stores, including but not limited to Charlene's Weather.

Data Collection

These apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are used.

Location Data

Some apps may request location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) solely to provide location-based features. Location data is used locally on your device and is never sent to any server controlled by the developer. You can always manually enter a location instead of using GPS.

Network Access

Internet access is used only to fetch the data the app displays (e.g., weather data from public APIs, web content you request).

Third-Party Services

When an app connects to a third-party service (e.g., National Weather Service API), it sends only the minimum data required for that request (e.g., coordinates, search text). Those services have their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Apps may store the following locally on your device only:

  • Preferences and settings (e.g., theme choice, saved location)
  • Cached data to improve load speed

No stored data ever leaves your device.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated date at the top will be revised.

Contact

Questions? Contact the developer via the contact information found within each app.
